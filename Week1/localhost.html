Container

A container is place to put all of your content and layouts. It adjusts with screen size allowing for responsive designs.

GKit has supports two screen sizes.

Small
Screen sizes less than 768px
Normal
Screen sizes greater than or equal to 768px

Grid

GKit has a column and row grid that supports the small and normal screen sizes.

GKit uses a 12 column system for normal and small screens. Normal will automatically collapse to block elements when the screen is smaller than 768px. Small will always appear horizontal.
